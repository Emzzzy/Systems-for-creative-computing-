Colour theory - 
- Complementary colours - colours that sit opposite each other on the colour wheel
- Analogous colours - Any three colours which are side by side on the 12 - section such as yellow-green, yellow, and yellow-orange
- Monochromatic colours - colours containing or using only one colour 
- Rectangle colour palette - four points on the colour wheel, works best when one colour is dominant
- Triadic colours - a palette based around a triangle, 3 points
